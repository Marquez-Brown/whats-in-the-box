How to use What's in the box?

    Creating the QR Label

  • Create your QR code by filling out all of the form's fields. Press the generate QR code button to generate a QR label that will contain all of your box contents.

    • My Boxes Page

  • The My Boxes page will store all of your boxes' contents and are only available with the device you made the QR code. Click on the box you need to find out what's in the box.

    • Find a Charity near you

  • While viewing the contents of your box, Press the "Find a local charity near you!" button and search for the zip code of the area you wish to donate to or press the use my location button to find a local charity within 10 miles of you.
